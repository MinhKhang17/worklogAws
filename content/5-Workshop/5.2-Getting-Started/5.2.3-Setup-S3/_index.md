If you are running this workshop using your own AWS account, follow the steps below to provision an S3 bucket.

---

1.  Go to the [AWS S3 console](https://s3.console.aws.amazon.com/s3/home) 

---

2.  Click **Create Bucket**

![services menu type Athena](/images/5-Workshops/5.2.3/1.png)

---

3.  Name your bucket `athena-iceberg-workshop-<random number>`. Keep in mind that bucket names must be globally unique. Leave all settings at their defaults, scroll to the bottom, and click **Create Bucket**.

![s3 bucket name](/images/5-Workshops/5.2.3/2.png)

---

**Well done!** Your S3 bucket has been successfully created.
