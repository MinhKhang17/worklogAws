### Overall Evaluation

**1. Working Environment**  
The working environment in the AWS First Cloud Journey program is open, supportive, and collaborative. Team members are approachable and always willing to help when I encounter difficulties. This creates a comfortable atmosphere that allows me to focus on my tasks. However, I believe the program could be improved by organizing more team bonding activities to strengthen connections among members.

**2. Support from Mentor / Team Admin**  
The mentor provides clear explanations and practical guidance, especially when working on the MyFit project. Instead of giving direct answers, the mentor encourages independent thinking, which helps me improve my problem-solving skills. The admin team is also supportive in providing necessary resources and ensuring smooth coordination throughout the program.

**3. Relevance of Work to Academic Major**  
The internship tasks are highly relevant to my academic background, particularly in software development and system design. Additionally, working with AWS services exposed me to cloud technologies, which are essential in modern IT environments.

**4. Learning & Skill Development Opportunities**  
During the internship, I significantly improved my technical skills, including programming, system design, and cloud service utilization. I also developed soft skills such as teamwork, communication, and report writing. Working on a real project like MyFit gave me valuable hands-on experience in building a cloud-based risk management system.

**5. Company Culture & Team Spirit**  
The team culture is professional yet friendly. Members respect each other and actively collaborate to solve problems. Even as an intern, I felt included and valued as part of the team, which motivated me to contribute more actively.

**6. Internship Policies / Benefits**  
The program provides a supportive learning environment along with opportunities to participate in training sessions related to AWS. Flexible working arrangements also help balance learning and productivity.

### Additional Questions

**• What did you find most satisfying during your internship?**  
The most satisfying aspect was being able to apply AWS services to a real project (MyFit) and see how theoretical knowledge can be transformed into a practical system.

**• What do you think the company should improve for future interns?**  
The program could improve by providing a more structured onboarding process and organizing additional team activities to enhance interaction among interns.

**• If recommending to a friend, would you suggest they intern here? Why or why not?**  
Yes, I would recommend this program because it offers practical experience, strong mentorship, and exposure to modern cloud technologies.

---

### Suggestions & Expectations

- The program could include more hands-on workshops focused on advanced AWS services.  
- It would be beneficial to have clearer project roadmaps at the beginning of the internship.  
- I would like to continue learning and potentially participate in similar programs in the future.  

**Other comments:**  
Overall, this internship was a valuable experience that helped me grow both technically and professionally.
